MAYDAY!

Catastrophic manmade fires are raging across the Brazilian Amazon, imperiling the world’s most biodiverse forest. The main reason of these fires is illegal deforestation and clearing of farmland. Also, in Brazil, weakened law enforcement has made things worse. Scientists, NGOs, and many global leaders largely agree that the real crisis is a political one.The worst may be yet to come. The current fires are actually burning in areas that were deforested back in May and June. Newly deforested areas will still burn in the coming months as many ivestigations say.

S.O.S

An endangered specie is a type of organism that is threatened by extinction. Species become endangered for two main reasons: loss of habitat and human activity. A loss of habitat can happen naturally, for instance from the hot atmoshere that the climate change has caused or the destruction of their place where they live which is for example burned, flattened and so on and is highly connected with humans because they destroy animals' houses for their own benefit.
